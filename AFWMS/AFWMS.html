Read our story

We are trying to make India hunger free.

Hunger is the most important problem still prevailing in the society as there are no schemes which support homeless people. There are both reported and unreported deaths and even suicides in many parts of the country due to hunger and malnourishment.

An organisation envisioned with bringing about zero food wastage, AFWMS. In our country, there are many places where food is overdue as leftovers. We help solve this problem by letting that overflown food to the underflowed, helping the unfortunate with nothing but the existing resource more efficiently.

We are a non-profit organisation intending to help become this world a better place. Lets help people not to go bed with empty stomachs. There is so much we can do make our society better. Let's be effective and efficient to make out the most. And yeah, giving opens the way for receiving!
